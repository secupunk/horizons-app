GOOGLE SEARCH CONSOLE SETUP GUIDE FOR CITYHEART

1. Access Google Search Console
   - Go to https://search.google.com/search-console

2. Add Property
   - Click "Add property" in the top left dropdown
   - Choose "Domain" property type (recommended)
   - Enter: cityheart.run
   - Click Continue

3. Verify Ownership
   - Copy the TXT record provided by Google
   - Log in to your domain registrar (where you bought cityheart.run)
   - Add a new DNS record:
     * Type: TXT
     * Name/Host: @ (or leave blank)
     * Value: [Paste the Google verification string]
   - Wait 5-10 minutes for DNS propagation
   - Click "Verify" in Google Search Console

4. Submit Sitemap
   - In GSC sidebar, click "Sitemaps"
   - Under "Add a new sitemap", enter: sitemap.xml
   - Click "Submit"
   - Verify status becomes "Success"

5. Ongoing Monitoring
   - Check "Pages" under Indexing to ensure pages are being crawled
   - Check "Page Experience" and "Core Web Vitals"
   - Monitor "Performance" for search queries and clicks
